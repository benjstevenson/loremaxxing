Here Be Legends

Most people leave this world without a single tale worth repeating. The loremaxxer does not. Below lie twelve sacred sidequests. Complete them. Then die interesting.

⚔ The Sacred Sidequests ⚔

  • I.
    Survive the Night in a Haunted Place
    Not a theme park. A real one. Alone. No cell service. Come back changed.
  • II.
    Start a Cult (a Good One)
    Gather people around a strange, sincere belief. Hold a ceremony. Make them wear something.
  • III.
    Get Lost in a Foreign Country on Purpose
    No map. No plan. Find shelter, food, and a story in 48 hours using only instinct and charm.
  • IV.
    Duel Someone (Legally)
    Chess. Arm wrestling. Formal debate. A cooking contest at high noon. The gauntlet must be thrown.
  • V.
    Leave an Anonymous Legend in Your City
    A mural. A plaque. A buried time capsule. Something people will wonder about for decades.
  • VI.
    Train With a True Master
    Find the best in the world at something obscure — falconry, swordplay, foraging — and apprentice under them.
  • VII.
    Give Something Away That Hurts to Lose
    Not money. Something irreplaceable. To a stranger. No tax receipt. No documentation. Just the act.
  • VIII.
    Survive 30 Days Without the Algorithm
    No social media. No streaming. No feeds of any kind. Read, walk, and remember how your mind felt before.
  • IX.
    Perform in Front of 100 Strangers
    Stand-up, spoken word, a magic trick, a terrible song. The medium doesn't matter. The fear does.
  • X.
    Build Something With Your Hands That Outlasts You
    A piece of furniture. A wall. A boat. Something you made from raw material that will exist when you don't.
  • XI.
    Cross a Border on Foot
    Legally. But slowly. Feel every step of the threshold. Let the geography of the world become real in your body.
  • XII.
    Tell Someone the Whole Truth
    The thing you've never said out loud. To the person who most needs to hear it. No context. No softening. Just the truth.
